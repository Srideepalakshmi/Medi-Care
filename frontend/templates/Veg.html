Greeny Vegetables!





Need assistance? Dr. SymptoCare is here to help you out!

Spinach

Spinach is a nutrient-rich leafy green vegetable loaded with iron, calcium, vitamins A, C, and K. It's excellent for boosting immunity and improving bone health.



Broccoli

Broccoli is a powerhouse of fiber, antioxidants, and vitamins. It helps in digestion, reduces inflammation, and supports heart and brain health.



Carrot

Carrots are packed with beta-carotene, which converts to vitamin A, promoting better vision, skin health, and a strong immune system.



Tomato

Tomatoes are rich in lycopene, a powerful antioxidant that supports heart health, improves skin texture, and reduces cancer risk.



Sweet Potato

Sweet potatoes are full of fiber, potassium, and vitamins, especially vitamin A. They help regulate blood sugar and improve gut health.



Cauliflower

Cauliflower is low in calories and high in fiber and vitamin C. It supports digestion and acts as an anti-inflammatory food.



Green Peas

Green peas are a great plant-based protein source and rich in fiber, iron, and vitamin K. They support blood sugar regulation and heart health.



Cabbage

Cabbage is known for detoxifying the body. It is high in vitamin C and fiber, helping with digestion and immune support.



Beetroot

Beetroot is great for increasing blood flow, lowering blood pressure, and boosting stamina, thanks to its natural nitrates.



Bottle Gourd

Bottle gourd is a hydrating vegetable that helps in weight loss, supports digestion, and cools the body during hot weather.
