Symptoms Analyzer!



Need assistance? Dr. SymptoCare is here to help you out!

Diabetes Analyzer


Causes: Genetics, obesity, lack of activity.
Symptoms: Thirst, frequent urination, tiredness, blurred vision.


Blood Pressure (BP) Analyzer


Causes: Stress, poor diet, dehydration.
Symptoms: Headache, dizziness, nosebleeds, fainting.


Fever-related Illnesses Analyzer


Causes: Mosquito bites, poor hygiene, infections,
Symptoms: High fever, chills, body pain, weakness.


Asthma Analyzer


Causes: Pollution, allergies.
Symptoms: Breathing trouble, chest tightness, coughing.


Thyroid Disorders Analyzer


Causes: Autoimmune issues, iodine deficiency.
Symptoms: Weight changes, fatigue(extreme tired), mood swings.


Heart Diseases Analyzer


Causes: High BP, cholesterol, smoking.
Symptoms: Chest pain, breathlessness, tiredness.


Kidney Diseases Analyzer


Causes: Diabetes, high BP, dehydration.
Symptoms: Swelling, low urine, back pain.


Liver Diseases Analyzer


Causes: Alcohol, virus, poor lifestyle.
Symptoms: Yellow skin, tiredness, stomach pain.


Anemia Analyzer


Causes: Poor diet, blood loss.
Symptoms: Pale skin, fatigue, short breath.


COVID-19 (Coronavirus) Analyzer


Causes: Viral infection.
Symptoms: Fever, loss of taste/smell, cough, tiredness


Skin Infections Analyzer


Causes: Poor hygiene, contact with infected person.
Symptoms: Itching, red patches, rashes.


Tuberculosis (TB) Analyzer


Causes: Bacteria (Mycobacterium TB).
Symptoms: Cough with blood, night sweats, weight loss.
