Chicken Breast

Chicken breast is a lean source of protein, low in fat, and rich in essential nutrients. Ideal for muscle building and weight loss.



Salmon

Salmon is high in omega-3 fatty acids, protein, and B vitamins. It supports heart health and brain function.



Eggs

Eggs are a complete protein source and contain all nine essential amino acids. They're also rich in choline and good fats.



Tuna

Tuna is low in calories but packed with protein and healthy fats. It helps with muscle recovery and brain health.



Lamb

Lamb is rich in high-quality protein, iron, and zinc. It supports red blood cell production and immune health.



Shrimp

Shrimp is low in calories and high in protein and iodine. It supports thyroid function and overall metabolism.



Turkey

Turkey is a lean meat loaded with protein, B vitamins, and selenium. It helps boost immunity and maintain energy levels.



Duck

Duck is flavorful and high in iron and protein. It's great for boosting energy and supporting muscle function.
