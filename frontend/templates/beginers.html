Exercises for Beginners





Need assistance? Dr. SymptoCare is here to help you out!

Jumping Jacks

A full-body warm-up exercise that increases your heart rate and warms up the muscles.

Wall Sit

Strengthens your legs and improves endurance by holding a seated position against a wall.

Knee Push-ups

Modified push-ups that build upper body strength without full bodyweight strain.

Glute Bridges

Targets your glutes and lower back; great for beginners to build core strength.

Bodyweight Squats

Basic lower-body move that strengthens thighs, hips, and glutes using just your weight.

Leg Raises

A simple core exercise that strengthens your lower abdominal muscles. Lie flat and slowly raise your legs upward while keeping them straight. Great for beginners.
