News

Here are some recent dangerous diseases and their preventive measures.

diabetes

Diabetes is a chronic disease that occurs when the body either doesn’t produce enough insulin or cannot effectively use the insulin it produces, leading to high blood sugar levels.

Prevention: Maintain a healthy lifestyle with a balanced diet, regular physical activity, and weight management to reduce the risk of diabetes.


covid-19 vaccine

COVID-19 is a contagious respiratory illness caused by the SARS-CoV-2 virus, leading to symptoms like fever, cough, and difficulty breathing, with severe cases requiring hospitalization.

Prevention: Follow hygiene measures like wearing masks, washing hands frequently, maintaining social distance, and getting vaccinated to reduce infection risk.

Dengue Fever

Dengue fever is a mosquito-borne viral infection causing high fever, severe body pain, and rash, with severe cases leading to bleeding or shock.

Prevention:Use insect repellent, wear protective clothing, and eliminate standing water to prevent mosquito breeding.
